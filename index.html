Tournament
Rules

Playson & Friends Malta Tournament starts on April 25th, 2025 at 20:00, and ends on April 25th, 2025, 23:00. 

To join the tournament, a player should use his/her own device.

Everyone can take part in the tournament for free, joining the gameplay at any moment during the tournament duration.

After registration, a player gets 5000 virtual coins balance and an unfixed number of spins.

The game is over when there is no balance left, or when the tournament time has expired.

A player gets points for winnings: for each 1.00 won, 1 point will be granted.

First 5 players with highest scores become winners and appear on the leaderboard.

Each of the top 5 winners will receive a prize after the tournament concludes. The 1st place winner will receive a PlayStation 5, the 2nd place will receive a bottle of Magnum champagne, and from 3rd to 5th place winners will receive a bottle of regular champagne.

Each player is allowed to use only one registered attempt. For the purpose of fair play leaderboard will be checked and moderated to ensure all winning places are captured by unique players.

The results will be announced promptly after the tournament has concluded.

It is the player’s responsibility to know and ensure that participation in the tournament is in line with the applicable legal conditions of the territory where he resides and that there are no restrictions applicable.
